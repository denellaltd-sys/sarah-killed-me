Privacy Policy - Sarah Killed Me!

Effective date: 3 July 2026

Sarah Killed Me! ("the app") is published by Denella Ltd (contact: denellaltd@gmail.com).

The short version

The app collects no personal data. Nothing you do in the app leaves your device.

Details

  • No accounts. The app has no sign-up, login, or user profiles.
  • No data collection. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage analytics, or device identifiers.
  • Local storage only. Your game progress (scores, streaks, current level, settings) is saved in the app's local storage on your device. It is deleted when you uninstall the app. We never see it.
  • Notifications. The app can show a daily local reminder that a new puzzle is available. This notification is scheduled entirely on your device; no server is involved. You can decline or disable notification permission at any time in your device settings.
  • No ads. The app contains no advertising and no third-party ad SDKs.
  • No third-party services. The app works fully offline.
  • Children. The app is a logic puzzle suitable for a general audience and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Future features

If a future update adds an optional online leaderboard, it will be opt-in, clearly labelled, and this policy will be updated before release.
