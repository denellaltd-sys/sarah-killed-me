Support — Sarah Killed Me!

Sarah Killed Me! is published by Denella Ltd. For any question, problem, or feedback, email contactus@denellapublishing.com — a human reads every message.

Reporting a problem

If something goes wrong, please tell us:

  • your phone or tablet model,
  • what you were doing when it happened,
  • the app version — shown in small print at the bottom of the home screen (the case desk).

If the app ever closes unexpectedly, simply open it again — it will quietly send us an anonymous report of what went wrong, and it can repair itself if the problem repeats. No action needed from you.

Common questions

  • I accused the wrong Sarah in Today's Case. That's the rule of the daily: one accusation, no second chances. A fresh case opens at midnight UTC.
  • My leaderboard name is taken. Each display name can play each day's case once, worldwide. Pick another name, or come back tomorrow.
  • How do I remove my name from the leaderboard? Email us the display name and we'll delete its entries — see the privacy policy for details.
  • Does the app need internet? Only for the ranked daily leaderboard. The Case Ladder and unranked daily play work offline.
